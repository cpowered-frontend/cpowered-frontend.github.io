Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is solar power installation ?

    A solar power installation is typically made up of solar panels and inverters. It may also include batteries to store solar energy to use when the sun is not shining.

  • Will having a solar power installation affect my normal electricity usage ?

    You will not feel any difference in the way you use electricity. The solar power installation integrates seamlessly with your existing power panel; so no need to do any switching! The only difference you will find is when your monthly electric bill slims down.

  • What happens after I click "Call Me" ?

    A crowd powered representative will get in touch with you to give you more information about the solar system. You will then tell us of a suitable time to conduct an inspection of your business or your home. Only then can we issue you with a formal offer and start the ball rolling on financing and building the system.

  • What type of financing is available to me ?

    A typical financial arrangement would be a lease to own scheme for businesses and a subsidized loan for homes. It is our experience however that there are no typical arrangements. We will agree with you on the best type of facility for your case.
